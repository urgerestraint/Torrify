# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Torrify is committed to a respectful, inclusive, and productive community.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and discuss ideas, not people.
- Provide actionable technical feedback.
- Help keep discussions on-topic and useful.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, abuse, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks, trolling, or intimidation.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Any conduct that makes participation unsafe.

## Scope

This policy applies in repository discussions, issues, pull requests, and project communication channels.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, remove, or moderate content that violates this code. Repeated or severe violations may result in temporary or permanent bans.

## Reporting

Report violations to <hello@torrify.org>.
